Welcome Back, !

This is your home page where you can manage your profile and settings.

  • Manage Cars: View and update your existing car listings. Make changes to car details, check availability, and ensure your inventory is up-to-date.
  • View Cars: Browse through the list of available cars in your inventory. Check the details of each car, including make, model, year, and rental status.
  • Register New Cars: Add new cars to your inventory. Enter details such as make, model, year, and other specifications to ensure your listings are complete and accurate.
  • Remove Cars: Remove cars from your inventory that are no longer available or need to be updated. This option helps keep your database clean and current.
  • Add to Rental: Manage car rentals by adding cars to the rental system. Set availability and rental terms to ensure efficient management of your fleet.
